# 引言
本文旨在根据论文整理自然语言处理中预训练技术的发展脉络及各模型的创新点。

1. NNLM: A Neural Probabilistic Language Model
2. Word2Vec: Efficient Estimation of Word Representations in Vector Space
3. GloVe: Global Vectors for Word Representation

5. ELMo: Deep contextualized word representations
6. GPT: Improving Language Understanding by Generative Pre-Training
7. BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers for Language Understanding
8. ERNIE:Enhanced Language Representation with Informative Entities (待读，加入了外部知识)
